Yoga Anywhere!

All our yoga classes includes Basic and advanced Yogasana practices, Pranayama, Meditation (Sanjeevani meditation, Cyclic meditation, Omkara meditation).

Online Courses

We offer and conduct regular online group/private yoga classes that are suitable for families and perfect for similar level yoga practitioners. These online group private yoga classes are useful for a group of regular yoga practitioners those who would like to practice together in small group and tailor make each class as per their level and goals. These classes are taught by highly experienced and qualified yoga masters who teach basic, intermediate and advanced poses (asanas), breathing techniques (Pranayamas), and meditation techniques, etc.

Our Approach to Asana Practice

A distillation of years of study and a lifetime of movement practice has moulded our approach to Vinyasa Flow and Hatha, which is an intelligent and transformational methodology that is easy to learn and teach, and helps people to go deep into their practice with lightness and grace. It is bio-mechanically savvy, features key actions for empowerment and freedom in the body, and is grounded in principles of sequencing that allow people to soar in every class.
